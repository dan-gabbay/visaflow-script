Resistance

"I need to consult with my wife" · 1–10 Method
4-Step Response
1
Validate
"Absolutely — after all, you travel together."
2
Reframe
"But let's assume your wife is in it with us — she's on board."
3
Temperature Check
"I want to understand from you — are you 100% confident in starting the process with us? let's say, on a scale of 1 to 10, where are you at?"
4
Respond to the Number
Based on their score, address the gap:
1 – 5
"What's holding you back? What would need to be true for you to feel fully confident?"
6 – 8
"You're close — what's the one thing stopping you from being a 10?"
9 – 10
"Perfect — let's not wait. Open your file now and loop your wife in on the details."
